─── SUBMITTING ───────────────────────────────────────────────

1. https://chrome.google.com/webstore/devconsole
2. Sign in. Pay the one-time $5 dev fee (credit card).
3. New Item → upload  dopafit-v0.1.0.zip
4. Fill the form:
        Name:               dopaFit
        Summary:            (paste from listing-copy.txt)
        Description:        (paste detailed description)
        Category:           Productivity
        Language:           English (United States)
        Privacy policy URL: (your hosted gist raw URL)
        Screenshots:        upload 3-5
5. Privacy practices section:
        ✓ "We use chrome.storage to save user data locally."
        ✗ Don't tick any data-sharing claims.
6. Submit.

Review takes 1-3 business days for first-time accounts.

─── KNOWN LIMITATIONS (be honest in launch posts) ─────────────

• Browser-only (no native Mac apps in v0.1 — bridge ships v0.2).
• Chrome desktop only (no iOS — Apple platform constraint).
• No sync across machines yet — data lives per-device.

These are all on the roadmap. The free, local, no-login posture is
deliberate, not a constraint.
